# ADR-0009: Keep repo/collateral as a scoped limitation in this cycle

**Date:** 2026-04-25T22:25:31.031359+00:00
**Recorded by:** codex
**Tags:** money-minsky-policy-mismatch, repo, collateral, scope

## Context

The factor-refinement cycle added sovereign_securities.py for maturity/rollover dynamics and external_constraint.py for import dependence and FX stress. With those higher-value qualifiers now modeled, the remaining question is whether this post also needs a dedicated repo/collateral/liquidity model.

## Decision

Do not build a repo/collateral/liquidity model in this cycle. Keep repo/collateral as explicit limitation language and operational context in the prose.

## Consequences

The post stays focused on first-order constraint diagnosis rather than funding-market microstructure. The rollover and external-constraint additions cover the omitted factors that most directly alter the main argument. Holder composition remains the highest-value remaining extension, and a future repo/liquidity model is justified only for a later post centered on crisis transmission, dealer balance sheets, haircuts, or safe-collateral shortages.
