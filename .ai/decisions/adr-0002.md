# ADR-0002: Simulation claims stay narrower than institutional thesis

**Date:** 2026-04-20T01:17:33.240106+00:00
**Recorded by:** codex
**Tags:** money-minsky-policy-mismatch, claim-boundaries, editorial

## Context

The post's core thesis is institutional and conceptual, but several older draft claims overstated what the models established.

## Decision

Allow strong claims about monetary position, sectoral logic, and policy mismatch, but keep simulation-derived claims calibration-specific, mechanism-level, and explicitly bounded by the audit.

## Consequences

Future revisions should weaken or remove any sentence that turns the models into universal proof. Section 7 limitations are part of the required shape of the post, not optional caveats.
