# ADR-0001: Money-Minsky draft uses rebuilt evidence chain

**Date:** 2026-04-20T01:17:33.235801+00:00
**Recorded by:** codex
**Tags:** money-minsky-policy-mismatch, evidence-chain, blog

## Context

The draft and repository still contain legacy SFC and ABM assets alongside newer rebuilt models and figures. Future sessions need to know which surface is authoritative.

## Decision

Treat policy_sectoral_model.py, austerity_counterfactual.py, and fragility_regimes.py as the accepted computational evidence chain for the current draft. Legacy SFC issuer/user outputs and ABM austerity-shift results are excluded from the active evidence chain until revalidated.

## Consequences

Future editorial and evidence passes should cite only the rebuilt package and its matching figures. Legacy figures may remain on disk but must not be used as support by default.
