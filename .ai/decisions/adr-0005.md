# ADR-0005: Money-Minsky board split into publish-track and research-track lanes

**Date:** 2026-04-20T03:25:58.747692+00:00
**Recorded by:** codex
**Tags:** money-minsky-policy-mismatch, board, priorities

## Context

The previous board grouped the current work into broad editorial/evidence/repro tasks plus a single low-priority legacy-repair task. Review of the draft and model package shows distinct needs: immediate coherence and validity work for publication, and separate simulation/model-development opportunities for future strengthening.

## Decision

Treat tasks 007-009 plus task-015 as the canonical publish track. Treat task-010 through task-014 as separate research-track work for validity strengthening and future simulations, especially fragility robustness, ABM redesign, SFC redesign, and an inflation/resource-boundary model.

## Consequences

Future sessions should not conflate publish-readiness with exploratory model development. The current draft can move toward publication through the publish-track tasks, while research-track tasks remain available to deepen or extend the argument without blocking publication.
